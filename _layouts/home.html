Think about the person who sits behind you in church, the receptionist you only see occasionally, or the spouse of a coworker you see once a year at the holiday party. NameTaggles helps you remember names and contact information with just a few taps on your phone.

Connect.

Connecting with people in 2020 is tricky, but now that you remember names and faces, Nametaggle provides tools to connect with people in your organization who share similar interests. With multiple social media and messaging platforms, people are overwhelmed by the constant barrage of messages and notifications. Why add more noise? Ours is not an app to share food pics or political thoughts and will not interrupt your day because someone you know liked or commented. Nametaggle is a place to interact with people and receive only information pertinent to you without pesky ads, likes or other social media pressures. Think of NameTaggle as social media for people who are tired of social media.

thumb_up

Engage.

Have you ever searched for an item online and the next day it appears in the ads on your social media feeds? Or have you expressed interest in an organization only to be bombarded by extraneous information on a daily basis? Nametaggle helps you engage with an organization and receive only the targeted information you desire. Within an organization you can sign up to receive notifications only from the groups you are interested in. Group leaders can engage members with instant information while maintaining user privacy.
