how_to_reg

Remember.

Have you ever been embarrased when you couldn't remember someone's name? It happens to everyone. NameTaggle started as a simple Pictoral Directory to help people remember each other. Imagine driving up to a remote office or meeting and a few taps and scrolls on your mobile device to get a reminder of everyone's name.

group

Connect.

Connecting with people in 2020 is tricky. Everyone uses a different messaging platform and is overwhelmed by the barrage of messages and notifications. So why add another platform? NameTaggle isn't intended to be a communication platform, rather it provides a contextual archive of information your members need when they need it.
