A new way to remember, connect and engage.


Remember.

Have you ever forgotten someone’s name? Think about the person who sits behind you in church, the receptionist you see occasionally, or the coworker’s spouse you see once a year at the holiday party. NameTaggle helps you remember names and contact information with just a few taps on your phone.

Connect.

Once you remember names and faces it is time to connect with others through our “Groups” feature. By joining only the groups within your organization that interest you, NameTaggle helps you interact with people and receive information pertinent to you without pesky ads, likes or other social media pressures.

Engage.

NameTaggle helps you filter the noise and engage with the parts of your organization that interest you. Instead of being inundated with messages and alerts, Nametaggle allows targeted notifications so you only hear from the groups with which you have already connected. Our Daily Digest provides the option for receiving notifications once a day, when you are ready.
