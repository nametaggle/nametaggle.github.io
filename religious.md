<h1 class="perm-marker"> Religious Organizations</h1>
*Because we created NameTaggle with religious organizations in mind, there are several unique ways for them to benefit from NameTaggle.*

##### FUMC Belmont, NC
When our church added a second Sunday morning service, we found that people attending one service weren't always able to meet and get to know new members and frequent visitors from the others. In NameTaggle, we were able to create groups for each service to help everyone get to know the new members and visitors.
